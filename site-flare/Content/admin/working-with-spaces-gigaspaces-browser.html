Working with Spaces

Users can configure and administer Spaces in the GigaSpaces Management Center. General maintenance activities, such as pinging, destroying, cleaning, stopping, starting, and restarting a Space are handled in the standard Space configuration area.

Use the advanced space configuration dialog to do the following:

  • Configure the general properties of a Space (retries, filters, lease manager, etc.)
  • Fine tune the Space performance (serialization, thread pool, etc.)
  • Perform memory management (configure cache size, policy, etc.)
  • Configure the JMS attributes
