Prerequisites - GigaSpaces-Excel Integration

 Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure:

  • GigaSpaces XAP.NET is Installed . Please note: the Excel Space Viewer supports only GigaSpaces version 6.5.1.

  • Microsoft Office Excel 2003 with Primary Interop Assemblies is installed.

  In case the Primary Interop Assemblies are not installed, install one of the following:

  • For Office 2003:

    • Microsoft Office Excel 2003 with Primary Interop Assemblies (requires the Office Installation CD).
    • Redistributable Primary Interop Assemblies Package for Office 2003 (does not require the Office Installation CD).
    • Update for Office 2003 (KB907417)

  • For Office 2007:

    • Redistributable Primary Interop Assemblies Package for Office 2007 (does not require the Office Installation CD).
